REST & RECOVER

Muscles don't grow in the gym — they grow while you rest. Today is not optional. It's when your body repairs the damage from 6 hard training days and comes back stronger.

  • ✓  Sleep 8–9 hours if possible
  • ✓  Light walk for 20–30 minutes (active recovery)
  • ✓  Stretching or yoga (15–20 min)
  • ✓  Foam rolling any sore muscles
  • ✓  Eat at maintenance (~2,450 kcal) — reduce carbs by 25%, keep protein at 130g
  • ✓  Drink 3+ litres water
  • ✓  Take weekly progress photo (front, side, back — same lighting)
  • ✓  Meal prep for the coming week
  • ✓  Review your workout log — did you progress this week?
Target: 2,750 kcal/day · 130g protein · 360g carbs · 80g fat · 6:00 AM training · 65 → 70 kg in 5–7 months
